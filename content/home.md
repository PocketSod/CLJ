Hero
======
Language Justice, Worker‑Owned in Indianapolis

We provide high-quality interpretation, translation, training, cultural brokering, and language-justice consulting to organizations across Indianapolis and Marion County — while creating sustainable livelihoods for language workers.

Primary CTAs: Get a Quote | Join the Co‑op

Mission Snapshot
-----------------
Our mission is to connect organizations with trusted language workers and to build an equitable, worker-owned cooperative that pays living wages and advances language justice.

- Quality interpretation and translation for community and institutions
- A cooperative model that centers language worker livelihoods
- Local focus: Indianapolis & Marion County

Services Overview
------------------
Interpretation
: On-site and remote interpretation for healthcare, legal, education, and community events.

Translation
: Document translation, localization, plain-language editing, and accessible formats.

Training
: Cultural competency, interpreter/translator professional development, and organization-facing language-justice workshops.

Cultural Brokering & Consulting
: Cross-cultural mediation, intake support, policy advising, and program design.
